Potato Soup


Description

This soup is not for those looking to stick to their diet. That being said, here, we're about enjoying food regardless of the calorie content. Warm and flavorful, this soup is sure to be a family favorite

This soup should take about 20 minutes to prepare, and another 30 minutes to cook. After a total of 50 minutes, you should have 8 delightful servings of potato soup!


Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups chicken stock, or enough to cover potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 3 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook the bacon over medium heat until done, and set aside. Drain off all but 1/4 cup of the bacon grease.
  2. cook celery and onion in the bacon grease until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic, and continue cooking for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Add cubed potatoes, toss to coat, and saute for 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Return bacon to the pan, and add enough chicken stock to just cover the potatoes. Cover, and simmer until potatoes are tender.
  6. In a separate pan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour, and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Whisk in the heavy cream, tarragon, and cilantro.
  8. Bring the cream mixture to a boil, and cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.
  9. Stir the cream mixture into the potato mixture.
  10. Puree about 1/2 the soup, and return to the pan. Adjust seasonings to taste.
