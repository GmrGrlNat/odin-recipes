Blueberry Muffins


Description

Who doesn't love a good ol' blueberry muffin for breakfast? Warm and fresh from the oven, covered with a cinnamon-sugar crumble topping, this recipe is sure to be a good start to your day.

This recipe takes about 15 minutes to prepare and 25 minutes to cook, totalling to only 40 minutes for 8 large muffins.


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit (200 degrees Celsius.) Grease muffin tin, or line with muffin liners
  2. In a large bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups flour, 3/4 cup sugar, the salt, and the baking powder.
  3. In a 1 cup or larger measuring cup, combine the oil and egg, and add enough milk to reach the 1 cup mark.
  4. Add the wet ingredient mixture to the dry ingredient mixture, and fold in the blueberries.
  5. To make the crumb topping: 1/2 cup sugar and 1/3 cup flour with the cubed butter and the cinnamon, and mix with a fork.
  6. Pour muffin batter into the tin, and sprinkle with the crumb topping
  7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until done.
